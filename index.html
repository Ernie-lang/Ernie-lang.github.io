Delicious Eclairs Recipe

Prep Time: 30 minutes | Cook Time: 25 minutes | Total Time: 55 minutes

Servings: 12 eclairs

Cuisine: French | Category: Dessert

Description

Learn how to make delicious eclairs with this easy recipe. These French pastries are light, airy, and filled with sweet cream, then topped with rich chocolate glaze.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 8 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 tbsp corn syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine butter, water, and salt over medium heat. Bring to a boil.
  3. Add flour and stir with a wooden spoon until mixture forms a ball and pulls away from the sides of the pan.
  4. Remove from heat and let cool for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add eggs one at a time, stirring well after each addition until dough is smooth.
  6. Transfer dough to a piping bag and pipe eclairs onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown. Let cool.
  8. In a medium bowl, whip heavy cream and powdered sugar until stiff peaks form.
  9. Cut eclairs in half and fill with whipped cream.
  10. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt chocolate chips and corn syrup in 30-second intervals, stirring until smooth.
  11. Stir in vanilla extract.
  12. Dip tops of eclairs into chocolate and let chocolate set.
  13. Serve and enjoy!

Nutrition Information

Per serving: 256 calories, 19g fat
